# Child Safety Standards

**CollectStuff** is committed to child safety and has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content.

## Our Standards

- We do not permit any content that sexualizes or exploits minors
- We prohibit grooming, sextortion, or trafficking of minors
- We do not allow sharing of child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- Our app does not include social features, messaging, or user-to-user content sharing

## Reporting

If you encounter any content or behavior that violates these standards, please report it immediately to:

- **Email:** support@butterlemonade.com
- **NCMEC CyberTipline:** [CyberTipline.org](https://www.cybertipline.org)

We cooperate fully with law enforcement in investigating any reported violations.

## App Description

CollectStuff is a single-player scavenger hunt game where users photograph everyday objects. The app does not include:
- User-generated content visible to others
- Social features or messaging
- Content sharing between users
- Features designed for or marketed to children
