CollectStuff Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2026

Introduction

CollectStuff ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile picture (if provided through social sign-in)

Camera and Photos

CollectStuff requires camera access to photograph items for your collection. We collect:

  • Photos you take within the app to document collected items
  • These photos are stored locally on your device and in your personal cloud storage

Important: We do not access your photo library. We only use the camera to capture new photos within the app.

Collection Data

We store information about items you collect, including:

  • Item names and categories
  • Date and time of collection
  • Photos associated with collected items

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the CollectStuff service
  • To authenticate your account
  • To store and display your collection
  • To improve our app and user experience

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.

  • Account data is stored in Firebase Authentication
  • Collection data is stored in Firebase Firestore
  • Photos are stored in Firebase Cloud Storage

Third-Party Services

CollectStuff uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase - For authentication, database, and storage
  • Google Sign-In - For optional account creation
  • Apple Sign-In - For optional account creation (iOS)
  • Facebook Login - For optional account creation
  • Google ML Kit - For on-device image recognition (processed locally, not sent to servers)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your collection data is private and only accessible to you.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your collection data

Children's Privacy

CollectStuff is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
