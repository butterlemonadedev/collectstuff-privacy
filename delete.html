Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Before you delete your CollectStuff account, please note the following:

This action is permanent

Deleting your account will permanently remove:

  • Your profile information
  • Your entire collection and photos
  • Your achievements and progress
  • All associated data

This cannot be undone.

Preferred Method: Delete in App

The fastest way to delete your account is through the app:

  1. Open CollectStuff
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm your identity
Can't access the app?
Use the form below to request account deletion.
